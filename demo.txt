[A regular presentation] A regular presentation
    Press <kbd>Space</kbd> to start
        Press <kbd>Space</kbd> or <kbd>←</kbd> <kbd>→</kbd> to move through the slides
        You started in overview mode, press <kbd>O</kbd> to return there
        In overview mode, click on a slide to jump to it
    But there's more
        [+] Press <kbd>+</kbd> now
            Sometimes a bullet point has a + after it
            When you press <kbd>+</kbd>, all the points with + unroll to become their own slides
            The original slide now serves as a section overview
            Try it again!
                right image:http://placekitten.com/400/400
                Return to overview mode to see your changes
                You can also unroll in overview mode
            Why is that useful?
                Enables you to choose: <q>Does this slide provide enough information for this audience? Or do I need to go into more detail?</q>
                High-level slides can set the scene
                [Deep dive] Deep dive into the topics that interest the audience
                    No need to customize presentations beforehand
                    No need for "just in case" slides
                    Easily skip over sections if time is short
                    Just bring all your slides and make decisions live
                    Text colour hints at your depth
            Press <kbd>−</kbd> to roll up one level of detail
    And more
        Presentations are self-contained HTML files <small>(apart from images and fonts)</small>
        So you can use them even if your venue has no Internet access
        Just click the Save button and save it to a memory stick
        note: HTML 5 in use! Best bring a recent version of Chrome or Firefox with you
    Features for power users
        Hit <kbd>Escape</kbd> to launch a basic editor for fixing typos <small>(very alpha)</small>
        Click the New buton in overview mode to save an empty presentation
        A blue border in overview mode warns you if a slide needs Internet access to render properly
